## DESCRIPCIÓN COMPLETA — INGLÉS

Stop scrolling through hundreds of AI conversations. Orenivo is a Chrome extension that helps you organize ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini and DeepSeek chats from one clean side panel.

★ WHY ORENIVO?

If you use AI daily, you know the pain: important conversations buried under dozens of random chats, no way to group them by project, and no fast way to find them again. ChatGPT's "Projects" feature only solves part of the problem. Claude has almost no organization. And if you use multiple platforms? Everything gets fragmented.

Orenivo fixes this. One extension, four platforms, zero chaos.

★ KEY FEATURES

▸ Folders & Subfolders — Drag and drop conversations into color-coded folders. Create nested subfolders for complex projects.

▸ 4 Platforms, 1 Panel — ChatGPT + Claude + Gemini + DeepSeek. Keep your organization in one shared side panel while you work across different AI tools.

▸ Title Search — Find conversations by title across all supported platforms from one place.

▸ Prompt Manager — Save your best prompts as reusable templates with dynamic variables like {topic} or {language}. Copy them quickly into any AI tool.

▸ Pin & Bookmark — Pin your most-used conversations to the top. Never lose that perfect debugging session or writing prompt again.

▸ Export & Backup — Download your entire folder structure as JSON. Your data, your control.

★ PRIVACY FIRST

Orenivo does NOT read, store, or transmit your conversation content. It reads conversation titles and related metadata needed to organize your chat list. No analytics trackers. No data selling.

★ WHAT IT'S GOOD FOR

• Organizing research, coding, writing, and work chats by project
• Finding old conversations without scrolling forever
• Keeping reusable prompts in one place
• Working across multiple AI tools without losing context

★ GETTING STARTED

1. Install Orenivo
2. Open ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini or DeepSeek
3. Click the Orenivo icon to open the side panel
4. Create your first folder and drag conversations in
5. That's it — your AI workspace is now organized

## Privacy Policy for Orenivo

Last updated: April 2026

### What data we collect

Orenivo collects the following data:
- **Conversation metadata**: We read conversation titles, platform identifiers, links, and timestamps needed to display your chats inside Orenivo. We do NOT read conversation content.
- **Folder structure**: Your folders, subfolders, color preferences, and conversation-to-folder assignments are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API.
- **Prompt templates**: Prompts you create in Orenivo are stored locally on your device so you can reuse them later.

### What data we do NOT collect

- We do NOT read, store, or transmit the content of your AI conversations.
- We do NOT collect browsing history.
- We do NOT use analytics trackers or third-party tracking scripts.
- We do NOT sell, share, or transfer any user data to third parties.
- We do NOT display advertisements.

### Data storage

- All current Orenivo data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API (`chrome.storage.local`).
- No conversation content leaves your browser.
- Orenivo does not currently require an account, cloud sync, or payment processing.

### Your rights (GDPR)

As a user based in the EU, you have the right to:
- Access your data
- Request deletion of your data
- Export your data (built-in export feature)
- Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@orenivo.ai

### Permissions explained

- **storage**: Store your folder structure and preferences locally
- **activeTab**: Read conversation titles from the current AI platform tab
- **sidePanel**: Display the Orenivo organization panel
- **Host permissions** (chat.openai.com, claude.ai, gemini.google.com, chat.deepseek.com): Access these specific sites to read conversation titles and related sidebar metadata

### Changes to this policy

We will notify users of any material changes through the extension's update notes and our website.
