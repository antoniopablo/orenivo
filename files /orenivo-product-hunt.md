## MAKER COMMENT (publicar inmediatamente después del launch)

Hey everyone! 👋

I'm Antonio, a solo developer from Spain. I built Orenivo because I had 200+ ChatGPT conversations and couldn't find anything.

The problem: every AI platform (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek) has zero conversation organization. No folders, limited search, no way to pin or group chats by project. And if you use multiple platforms? Good luck.

**What Orenivo does:**
→ Adds a side panel to your browser with folders, subfolders, and colors
→ Search conversation titles across all supported platforms
→ Pin your most important chats
→ Prompt Manager with reusable templates and variables
→ Works on ChatGPT + Claude + Gemini + DeepSeek (no other tool covers all 4)

**Tech decisions I'm proud of:**
- Chrome Side Panel API instead of DOM injection = never breaks when platforms update
- Platform adapter pattern = adding a new AI service takes ~2 hours

**Current version:** local-first, no account required, data stays in your browser

Built in 4 weeks with €5 total investment. AMA in the comments!
