FAQ

Common questions

Does Orenivo read my conversations?
No. Orenivo only reads the titles of your conversations — never the content. Your messages, prompts, and responses remain entirely private. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.
Where is my data stored?
Free tier data lives exclusively on your device in Chrome's local storage — nothing leaves your browser. Pro tier cloud sync data is stored in Supabase on EU-based servers, encrypted at rest and in transit.
Does it work if I'm logged into multiple AI platforms at the same time?
Yes. You can be logged into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, and DeepSeek simultaneously in different tabs. Orenivo reads from all active tabs and shows everything in one unified panel.
Will it break when ChatGPT updates their UI?
Orenivo uses multiple selector fallbacks per platform and is updated whenever a major AI platform changes its UI. If something breaks, you'll see a refresh nudge in the panel.
Can I export my folders and prompts?
Yes. Free users can export a JSON backup from Settings. Pro users get one-click export and can import it on any other device.
What's the difference between Free and Pro?
Free gives you unlimited conversations, search, and 5 folders. Pro removes all limits and adds cloud sync across devices. Most solo users are happy on the Free tier.
