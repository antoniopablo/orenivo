Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Orenivo is a Chrome extension that helps you organize AI conversations. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data we collect

Free tier (local only): All data — folders, conversation titles, URLs, and prompt templates — is stored exclusively in your browser using Chrome's local storage (chrome.storage.local). Nothing is sent to our servers.

Pro tier (cloud sync): If you sign up for a Pro account, we store the following on our servers:

  • Your email address (used for authentication)
  • Conversation titles and URLs (not the content of conversations)
  • Folder names and structure
  • Prompt template names and text
  • Your subscription plan and status

We never access, store, or transmit the actual content of your AI conversations.

2. How we use your data

  • To sync your folders and settings across devices (Pro only)
  • To manage your subscription and billing via Lemon Squeezy
  • To send you a one-time verification code when you sign in

We do not sell your data, share it with third parties for advertising, or use it for any purpose other than providing the service.

3. Data storage and security

Pro tier data is stored on Supabase, hosted in the EU. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit (TLS). Each user can only access their own data — enforced at the database level with Row Level Security.

4. Third-party services

  • Supabase — authentication and database (EU servers)
  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing. We share your email with Lemon Squeezy only when you purchase a Pro subscription.

5. Data retention and deletion

Free tier: data stays in your browser until you uninstall the extension or clear browser data.

Pro tier: you can export and delete all your data at any time from the extension's Settings page. To permanently delete your account and all associated data, email us at support@orenivo.com.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, you have the right to access, correct, export, and delete your personal data. Contact us at support@orenivo.com for any requests.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@orenivo.com.
