Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: Orenivo reads conversation titles and a small amount of related metadata needed to organize your chat list — never the message content. Everything is stored locally on your device. We don't track you, sell data, or use advertising. Ever.

1. What Orenivo does

Orenivo is a Chrome extension that organizes your AI conversations from ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and DeepSeek into a side panel with folders, search, and prompt templates. It reads minimal conversation metadata from those pages and stores it locally in your browser.

2. Data we collect

Data What we do with it Where it's stored
Conversation metadata Display chats in the side panel for organization and reopening Your device only (chrome.storage.local)
Folder structure Remember your folder organization Your device only
Prompt templates Save and reuse prompts you create Your device only

3. What we never collect

  • The content of your AI conversations (messages, responses)
  • Browsing history outside of AI platforms
  • Personal information such as email, name, or payment details
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Analytics or behavioral tracking data

4. Permissions explained

Permission Why we need it
storage Store your folder structure and settings locally in Chrome
sidePanel Display the Orenivo panel in Chrome's native side panel
activeTab Read conversation titles and related metadata from the currently active AI tab
Host permissions
(chatgpt.com, claude.ai, etc.)		 Access the sidebar DOM of these specific sites to extract conversation titles and related sidebar metadata

5. Third-party services

Orenivo does not currently use third-party analytics, advertising, payment processors, or cloud sync services.

Orenivo does not use Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or any behavioral analytics service.

6. Data storage and security

All data lives exclusively in Chrome's chrome.storage.local API on your device. No data ever leaves your browser.

You can export or delete your data at any time from the Settings panel.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of your data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Right to portability — export your data via the built-in JSON export
  • Right to object — object to data processing
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@orenivo.ai.

8. Data retention

Data is stored on your device and persists until you uninstall the extension, import over it, or clear Chrome storage.

9. Children's privacy

Orenivo is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the extension evolves. Material changes will be communicated via the Chrome extension update notes. The date at the top of this page reflects the latest revision.
